# Project 05 – Microsoft Teams Administration

## Overview

This project demonstrates practical Microsoft Teams administration within a Microsoft 365 Business Premium environment.

The lab focused on Teams user management, creating and managing a private team, configuring ownership and membership, reviewing guest access, and examining organization-wide meeting, messaging, and Teams policies.

---

## Scenario

An organization's IT department requires a dedicated Microsoft Teams workspace for its support staff.

As the Microsoft 365 administrator, the task is to create an IT Support Team, configure appropriate ownership and membership, review guest access, and examine the policies that control meetings, messaging, and Teams functionality across the organization.

---

## Objectives

- Navigate the Microsoft Teams Admin Center
- Review Teams-enabled users
- Create a private Microsoft Team
- Configure team ownership
- Manage team membership
- Configure a group email address
- Review guest access
- Review meeting policies
- Review messaging policies
- Review Teams policies

## Lab Steps

1. Accessed the Microsoft Teams Admin Center.
2. Reviewed users available within the Teams environment.
3. Reviewed the Teams configuration of a lab user.
4. Created a private team named `IT Support Team`.
5. Configured the group email address for the team.
6. Assigned an administrator account as the team owner.
7. Added lab users as team members.
8. Verified team ownership and membership.
9. Reviewed existing guest users and guest access configuration.
10. Reviewed the Global (Org-wide default) meeting policy.
11. Reviewed the Global messaging policy.
12. Reviewed the Global Teams policy.

## Lessons Learned

- Microsoft Teams can be centrally administered through the Teams Admin Center.
- Teams use Microsoft 365 identity and group services for membership and collaboration.
- Owners and members have different responsibilities within a Team.
- Private Teams restrict access to approved members.
- Guest access enables controlled collaboration with external users.
- Meeting and messaging policies allow administrators to control functionality across the organization.
- Organization-wide policies provide centralized and scalable management of Teams functionality.
- Teams administration is an important component of modern Microsoft 365 IT support.
